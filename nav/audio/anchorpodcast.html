This audio is the recorded, audio-only version of The H.B. Sunrise Podcast, a Videogame, Technology, and Pop Culture podcast that is broadcast live on Twitch.tv and Mixer.com, and at the same time, recorded and uploaded as a video to YouTube. When this is all said and done, I use Audacity to increase listenability, as well as put in pre-recorded advertisement breaks with music I created myself in FL Studio. These podcasts are uploaded to a tool called Anchor, which then distributes the podcast to dozens of online music and audio distributers, including Spotify, iTunes, and Last.fm.
